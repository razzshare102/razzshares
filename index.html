❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RazzShares Web3.

What is RazzShares Web3?
RazzShares Web3 is a premium crypto community focused on airdrop hunting, alpha sharing, and Web3 education. We aggregate the best opportunities, research projects early, and share insights across Telegram, Discord, Twitter, and YouTube to help our members grow their portfolios.
Is RazzShares free to join?
Yes! Our core community channels are completely free to join. We believe quality crypto education and alpha should be accessible to everyone. We do have optional premium tiers for exclusive early-access alpha and personalized research, but the free community alone provides enormous value.
What is InfoFi and how does RazzShares use it?
InfoFi (Information Finance) is the emerging paradigm of monetizing and incentivizing high-quality information sharing in Web3. RazzShares creates InfoFi content by producing research, tutorials, airdrop guides and alpha calls — content that has direct financial value to our community members and has helped them collectively earn millions.
How do I participate in airdrop hunting?
Join our Telegram channel to receive daily airdrop alerts. We post step-by-step guides for each opportunity including: which wallet to use, what transactions to execute, testnet participation tips, and estimated reward potential. We cover 50+ chains and track hundreds of upcoming token launches simultaneously.
What kind of alpha does the community share?
Our alpha ranges from early-stage project discoveries, VC funding rounds before announcements, testnet incentive programs, upcoming TGEs (Token Generation Events), smart money wallet tracking, narrative plays, and on-chain analytics insights. Quality over quantity is our motto — we only share what our team has personally vetted.
Are the alpha calls financial advice?
No. All content shared by RazzShares Web3 is for informational and educational purposes only. Nothing constitutes financial advice. Crypto markets are highly volatile — always do your own research (DYOR), understand the risks, and never invest more than you can afford to lose. Our goal is to educate and inform, not to direct financial decisions.
How can I contribute to the community?
We love active contributors! Share your own findings in our channels, participate in discussions, write research pieces, create tutorials, or translate content for non-English speaking members. Top contributors are recognized with special roles and sometimes rewarded with exclusive perks including early access to premium content.
